Sanuki Udon









Kagawa is the one place in Japan that produces high quality flour and hence this prefecture is known for this udon dish. The udon noodles have the right springy texture and the delicious broth is made from fresh anchovies from the Seto Inland Sea. If you can't make it all the way to Japan for some authentic sanuki udon, we hear that there are now restaurants outside of Japan that serve some pretty good sanuki udon too!
